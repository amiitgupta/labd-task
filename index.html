Frequently Ask Questions
  • What is the Labdox Campus Ambassador Program about?
    Labdox is a sought after space for science and technology enthusiasts to explore, seek & connect over scientific tools, equipment & concepts. We provide curated and customized training by giving onsite and real exposure in a workshop, laboratory or factory settings, as well as remote learning of trending concepts.
  • Who can apply?
    Anyone who meets the below criteria:
    • Minimum 18 years of age
    • Graduate and post-graduate students, currently pursuing their degree
  • How do I apply? What happens after I apply?
    You can apply directly through our website using the ‘Apply Now’ button on this page. Once you apply, you will hear from us if you are selected.
  • Can students from any college/university apply?
    Yes, students from any college & university can apply for the Labdox Campus Ambassador Program
  • How long is the Campus Ambassador Program?
    The program is for a duration of 3 months.
  • What are the responsibilities of a campus ambassador?
    As a Campus Ambassador for Labdox, you are required to do the following:
    • Endorsing the company- Promote and spread the cause of Labdox in your campus
    • Communicate and connect with students-to build our community in your respective campuses
    • Plan and organize different campaigns and programs
    • Spread awareness about the courses and assist them related queries
    • To frame and identify potential partnerships with student bodies in their respective campus
    • Collect feedback and Draft periodic reports
    • Free Courses
  • Will Labdox support the ambassadors in campaigns and activities they execute?
    Yes, Labdox will provide adequate resources and guidance for successful execution of planned marketing activities.
  • Are there any incentives for being a student ambassador?
    Yes, Labdox Campus Dockers Program provides an opportunity to gain experience, expand your connections internationally and learn new skills. We are looking for high spirited, social, and exuberant students with a passion for learning who can work with us to spread awareness and are creative enough to build and manage collaborations and marketing events.
  • Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding the Campus Ambassador Program?
    You can contact us at campus-ambassador@labdox.com
