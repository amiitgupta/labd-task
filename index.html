Frequently Ask Questions
  • What is the Labdox Campus Ambassador Program about?
    Labdox Campus Dockers Program provides an opportunity to gain experience, expand your connections internationally and learn new skills. We are looking for high spirited, social, and exuberant students with a passion for learning, who can work with us to spread awareness and are creative enough to build and manage collaborations and marketing events.
  • Who can apply?
    Anyone who meets the below criteria:
    • Students pursuing graduation and who are graduated.
    • Students pursuing post-graduation
  • How do I apply? What happens after I apply?
    You can apply by filling out the form Application Form
    For more information go to Our Website More information
  • What are the requirements for the program?
    Requirements for the Labdox Campus Ambassador Program :
    • Strong communication and presentation skills
    • Active in Social Media
    • Enthusiastic student involved in campus activities – clubs, fests, or social scenes.
    • Passionate to learn
  • How long is the Campus Ambassador Program?
    The program is for a duration of 3 months.
  • What are the responsibilities of a campus ambassador?
    As a Campus Ambassador for Labdox, you are required to do the following:
    • Endorsing the company- Promote and spread the cause of Labdox in your campus
    • Communicate and connect with students-to build our community in your respective campuses
    • Plan and organize different campaigns and programs
    • Spread awareness about the courses and assist them related queries
    • To frame and identify potential partnerships with student bodies in their respective campus
    • Collect feedback and Draft periodic reports
  • What are the additional benefits that a Campus Docker can acquire from the program?
    • Hands-on-experience: Campus Docker helps you to gain hands-on-experience in Online Marketing, Traditional Marketing and Sales.
    • Certificate of Internship
    • Networking: You will get direct exposure to the corporate world and a chance to build meaningful connections for your professional network.
    • Access to courses: You will have access to our skilfully crafted courses which can help you grow in this fast-moving world.
  • Are there any incentives for being a student ambassador?
    Yes, Labdox provide incentives to the Campus Docker. Based on planned marketing skills, leads generated and sales initiated, performance stipend will be provided to the Campus docker.
  • Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding the Campus Ambassador Program?
    You can contact us at aspire@labdox.in or www.labdox.com
